Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our furniture and services

How long will it take to receive my furniture?

Most in-stock items ship within 3-5 business days. Custom furniture typically takes 4-8 weeks. You'll receive tracking information once your order ships.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! Free standard shipping on orders over $500 within the continental US. Orders under $500 have a $49 shipping fee.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for standard furniture items in original condition. Custom pieces are non-returnable unless damaged or defective.

Do your products come with a warranty?

Yes, most furniture includes a limited warranty: frames (5 years), cushions (2 years), leather (3 years), and mechanisms (3 years).

Do you offer furniture assembly?

Yes, professional assembly is available in most areas for an additional fee. You can select this option during checkout.

Can I request fabric swatches?

Absolutely! Request up to 5 complimentary swatches through our website or customer service. They typically arrive within 3-5 business days.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Financing options through Affirm and Klarna are also available.
